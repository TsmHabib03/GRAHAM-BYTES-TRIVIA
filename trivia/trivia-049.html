#049 Cybersecurity

What does the "S" in HTTPS stand for?

Did You Know?

HTTPS uses SSL/TLS encryption to secure the connection between your browser and the website. The padlock icon in your browser's address bar means the site uses HTTPS. Always check for this when entering passwords or payment information!
